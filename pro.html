A modern approach to Italian cuisine in India
historic Jamshedpur

The Divine is a Italian restaurant with an emphasis on quality ingredients and precision cooking.
The vegetable-forward dishes pay tribute to the food of Milan with a menu that utilizes the bounty of India
produce and pays careful attention to detail – treating quality ingredients with respect
and allowing them to shine in their best iterations.

Given the global threat of COVID-19 to our community both near and far, we are enforcing and practicing
the highest sanitation and food safety protocols across the board. Our goal
is to continue to provide a service to our community, to offer security
to our staff, and to support our local food sources and suppliers.

A love letter to Italy woven from a different cloth

It was this skill, the reliance on hard work done by hand, that led us to find our own passion in food, heavily influenced
by our mother – an excellent cook, who fed an Italian diet of fresh vegetables, locally-caught seafood and homemade pasta.
Her meals were simple, prepared quickly, and always delicious.


Featuring a menu of fresh pastas, risotto, antipasti and crostini,
an extensive wine list highlighting the best organic and biodynamic varietals from Italy, and world class cocktails from the bar,
we invite you in to experience “la dolce far niente” at The Divine.
